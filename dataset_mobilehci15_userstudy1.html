MobileHCI'15 - UserStudy 1: EyeTab Accuracy

Study Details

This study assessed the accuracy of the EyeTab Computer Vision based gaze tracking approach.

Dataset Details

Each dataset contains a video that was produced of single photos taken during the user study as well as the raw taken photos. The name of the datasets indicate the settings that were used during the test run. The distance between a user and the tablet is indicated by the words normal (30cm) and small (20cm). The lighting condition is indicated by the letters lc (light ceiling), lw (light wall) and lb (light both). For more information, we refer to the above mentioned paper.
